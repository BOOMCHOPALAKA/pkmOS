Simple structure. No deep folders. Links do the organizing.

noteOS uses a flat folder structure on purpose. You don't organize by nesting folders ten levels deep. You organize by linking notes to each other. Here's what the vault looks like.

Raw inputs. Meeting transcripts, PDFs, images, documents you haven't processed yet. Drop files here and tell your AI what to do with them.
