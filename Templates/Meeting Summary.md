# Meeting Summary

*You don't need to use this directly. Just drop a transcript in chat (or in Downloads/) and tell AI to summarize it. AI reads this template and knows how to structure the output. What's below is the format it follows.*

---

### Overview
One to two sentences on what the meeting was about and why.

### [Topic Name]
One section per main topic discussed. Within each, cover what applies:

- **What was discussed** — key points, arguments, or data. Keep it tight.
- **Decision** — what was decided and why. Skip if no decision was reached.
- **Action items** — state the problem or issue first, then the tasks. Include who owns each and any deadlines.

Action items should lead with the "why" — the reader should understand what's driving the task before seeing what the task is.

**Good:**
> **Vendor delivery delays** (two shipments missed in January, impacting production timeline)
> - Sarah: follow up with vendor on revised schedule — by Friday
> - Marcus: identify backup vendor options — present at next standup

**Not this:**
> - Sarah: call vendor
> - Marcus: find other vendors

### Unresolved Issues
Anything still open across all topics. Flag what's blocking progress.

### Next Steps
The highest-priority actions moving forward.
